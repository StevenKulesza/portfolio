Reader,

I was born in a test tube and have no belly button for starters… Currently, Illustration is a huge part of my life. I spend most of my free time drawing underground comix, like artists similar to Robert Crumb, S. Clay Wilson and RK Sloane. I've even had a design printed by a skateboard company, as well as numerous bands. I’ve traveled to just about every state playing in bands. For over a year, I lived in a room the size of one and a half twin mattresses. I collect 80s model Tech Decks (Powell-Peralta, Hosoi, Bones, H-Street, ect). I really want to one day live on a sailboat stopping at ports around the world. I've created my own music magazine and plenty of smaller zines... I was just kidding about the test tube thing... but I really do not have much of a belly button.
